Learning from limited data in NLP

Google Research, Brain Team: Jason Wei, Xuezhi Wang, Dale Schuurmans, Maarten Bosma, Brian Ichter, Fei Xia, Ed H. Chi, Quoc V. Le, Denny Zhou

This paper investigates the ability of language models to perform few-shot prompting for reasoning tasks
